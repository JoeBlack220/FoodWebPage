Microlepidotus inornatus T. N. Gill, 1862 (wavyline grunt). Microlepidotus is a genus of grunts native to the Pacific coast of North America. Haemulidae is a family of fishes in the order Perciformes known commonly as grunts. There are about 133 species in 19 genera. These fish are found in tropical fresh, brackish, and salt waters around the world.
