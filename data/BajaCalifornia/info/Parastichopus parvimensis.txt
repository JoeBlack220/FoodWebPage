The warty sea cucumber is a Pacific species of sea cucumber that can be found from the Baja California Peninsula, Mexico, to Monterey Bay, California, USA, although only scattered individuals were reported to occur north of Point Conception, California. It is found mainly in low energy environments from the intertidal zone down to 30 m, but can occur as deep as 60 m. It has a soft, cylindrical body, with red-brown to yellowish leathery skin.
