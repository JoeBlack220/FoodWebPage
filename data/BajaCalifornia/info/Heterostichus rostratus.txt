The Giant Kelpfish is a species of clinid native to the west coast of North America where it is found from California to southern Baja California. It inhabits rocky areas with kelp and other large seaweeds. Its diet consists of small crustaceans, mollusks and fishes. This species can reach a maximum size of 61 centimetres TL and has been reported to live for 4 years. It can also be found in the aquarium trade. It is currently the only known member of its genus.
