Caulolatilus affinis T. N. Gill, 1865 (Bighead tilefish). Caulolatilus is a genus of tilefishes native to the Pacific and Atlantic coasts of the Americas. Tilefishes are mostly small perciform marine fish comprising the family Malacanthidae. They are usually found in sandy areas, especially near coral reefs. Commercial fisheries exist for the largest species, making them important food fish.
