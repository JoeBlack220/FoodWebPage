Tegula is a genus of small to medium-sized sea snails, marine gastropod molluscs in the family Tegulidae. Tegulidae is a family of small to large sea snails, marine gastropod mollusks in the superfamily Trochoidea. Trochoidea is a superfamily of small to very large vetigastropod sea snails with gills and an operculum. Species within this superfamily have nacre as the inner shell layer.
