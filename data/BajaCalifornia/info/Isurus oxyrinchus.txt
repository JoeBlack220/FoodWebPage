The shortfin mako shark , also known as the blue pointer or bonito shark, is a large mackerel shark. It is commonly referred to as the mako shark, as is the longfin mako shark . The shortfin mako is on record as the fastest-swimming shark, capable of bursts of speed up to 18.8 metres per second. The species is classified as Endangered by the IUCN. The shortfin mako shark is a fairly large species of shark.
