Polyplectropus puerilis is of family Polycentropodidae. The Polycentropodidae are a family of trumpet-net and tube-making caddisflies. The caddisflies, or order Trichoptera, are a group of insects with aquatic larvae and terrestrial adults. The aquatic larvae are found in a wide variety of habitats such as streams, rivers, lakes, ponds, spring seeps and temporary waters.
