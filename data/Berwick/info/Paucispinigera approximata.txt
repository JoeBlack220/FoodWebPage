Paucispinigera approximata is a species of ulidiid or picture-winged fly in the genus Pseudotephritis of the family Ulidiidae. The Ulidiidae or picture-winged flies are a large and diverse cosmopolitan family of flies, and as in related families, most species are herbivorous or detritivore. They are often known as picture-winged flies, along with members of other families in the superfamily Tephritoidea that have patterns of bands or spots on the wings.
