Achnanthes lanceolata is a freshwater species of algae of the genus of Navicula.Navicula is a genus of boat-shaped diatom algae, comprising over 1,200 species. Navicula is Latin for "small ship", and also a term in English for a boat-shaped incense-holder. Diatoms eukaryotic, primarily aquatic, single-celled photosynthetic organisms play an important role in global ecology.
