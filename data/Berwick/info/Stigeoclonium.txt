Stigeoclonium is a genus of green algae in the family Chaetophoraceae. Chaetophoraceae is a family of green algae in the order Chaetophorales. The green algae are a large, informal grouping of algae consisting of the Chlorophyta and Charophyta/Streptophyta, which are now placed in separate divisions, as well as the potentially more basal Mesostigmatophyceae, Chlorokybophyceae and Spirotaenia.
